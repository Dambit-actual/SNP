Our National Agenda
Who We Are

A New Force
for Australia

The Sovereign National Party stands as a vanguard for Australia's autonomy, heritage, and future. We advocate for policies that safeguard our nation's independence, ensure economic vitality, and promote social unity.

In an era where the interests of the Australian people have been sidelined, the SNP exists to realign our nation's course — with transparency, integrity, and absolute dedication to the Australian public.

Our mission is to preserve public assets as national treasures, stewarded for the collective benefit of all Australians, now and into the future.

Core Ideologies

What We
Stand For

Four founding principles guide every decision, every policy, and every action taken by the Sovereign National Party.

National Sovereignty
Australia's sovereign right to govern its own affairs — controlling our borders, making independent economic decisions, and preserving our cultural heritage — is non-negotiable.
Economic Prosperity
A dynamic open-market economy underpinned by prudent fiscal policies, minimal regulatory burdens, and strong support for entrepreneurship and free enterprise.
Social Cohesion
Unity and a shared sense of purpose strengthen our nation. We commit to a cohesive society that values respect, mutual understanding, and our collective heritage.
Responsible Governance
Integrity, transparency, and accountability are the cornerstones of the SNP. The interests of the Australian people are always — and unapologetically — at the forefront.
Our Platform

The Agenda for
a Sovereign Australia

01
Economy & Family
Abolish personal income tax. Zero-interest family loans for homes and vehicles. Incentives for first-time buyers and local industry. Australians keep more of what they earn.
02
Border Security
Robust border protection is paramount to sovereignty. Advanced surveillance, enhanced enforcement, and immigration managed strictly in the national interest.
03
Infrastructure & Liveability
No government should grow the population faster than it grows the systems that serve it. Housing, healthcare, schools, and transport lead population policy.
04
National Manufacturing
Revitalise Australian industry. Fair duties on imports. Prioritise Australian-made products. Ensure foreign entities contribute equitably to the market they profit from.
05
Healthcare
Healthcare is a right for Australian citizens. Expand the state-funded hospital network. Subsidise essential treatment. Prioritise the people who built and fund this nation.
06
National Defence
Reinstate mandatory national service for males 17–35. A well-prepared Australia is a secure Australia. Full support for returned and serving servicemen and women.
07
Education
Restore literacy, numeracy, and science as core objectives. Mandatory Australian history and civics. Massively expand TAFE and vocational pathways equal to university.
08
Law & Order
Decisive action on rising crime. Youth accountability from age 12. Immediate deportation of non-citizens convicted of offences. Community safety is not negotiable.
09
Australian Labour Corps
A permanent national civilian institution for civic service, emergency response, rehabilitation, and a recognised pathway to citizenship — contributing directly to national resilience.
